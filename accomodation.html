ACCOMODATION

CIKM 2019 will be held in Beijing, China. The venue of the conference is the China National Convention Center, which is China's largest and newest international conference center with primary location and completed facilities. We recommend two hotels that are within 10-mintue walking distance to the conference venue. We will release more information about the reservation details and conference room rate soon.

    • Summary
      CNCC Grand Hotel is adjacent to China National Convention Center. With the help of skybridge between the Hotel and the Center, it takes only 3 minutes to walk to the Center.The Hotel is also within walking distance to the Bird Nest (China National Stadium), the Water Cube (National Aquatics Center) and the State Indoor Stadium all of which are key Olympic venues. It's about 26 km (16 miles) to the Beijing Capital International Airport and 8km (5 miles) to Tian'an Men Square, heart of Beijing.
