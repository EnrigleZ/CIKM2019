PRESENTATION INSTRUCTIONS

Please find instructions on your presentations/posters below and additional details may come before the conference.
  • Each accepted Long research paper or Applied research paper has 15 minutes for presentation and 3 minutes for Q&A.
  • Each accepted Short research paper has 7 minutes for presentation and 3 minutes for Q&A.
  • Each accepted Demo paper has 4 minutes for presentation.
  • All accepted Long, Applied and Short research papers will have a poster in CIKM19. The size of posters should be 120cm(height)*80cm(width).
  • There is NO laptop in the room, so please bring yours. Some adaptors will be available, but it is preferred that you bring your own given the multitude of available adaptors.
  • Please arrive early to check if things can run smoothly.
  • Wi-Fi is available in all rooms. Please be sure all presenters download any videos before their session and do not depend on the Wi-Fi to run their presentations.
